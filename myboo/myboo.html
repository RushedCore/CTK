My Birthday: November 29th 2004

I was born on 11/29/2004, and that entire month the number one song per the Billboard Hot 100 was "My Boo" from Usher and Alicia Keys, the music video to which is embedded to the right.

With no idea what to put on this page, I figure the best I can do is give my opinion on the song, which you can read below.

So, at first, when I looked this up, I had no idea what song this was, and considering it was written when I was a baby, I think that's understandable. Then I started watching the music video, and I found myself disliking it. I couldn't relate to the subject matter, and it felt incredibly objectifying. I was convinced this would be bland party music that I would forget listening to once the song was over.

Then the music stopped.

Usher sits on the couch in a dimly lit room and just sits for a bit, and then the song just switches and becomes a love song, playing off of the sexual nature of the intro to show the difference between it and the far deeper, more romantic feelings he has for this woman.

From there, the chorus picked up, and then I recognized the song, but I was still focused on that moment of silence between the intro and the real song, and now I imagine this song will stick in my head much longer, simply because of how well that moment challenged my expectations.
